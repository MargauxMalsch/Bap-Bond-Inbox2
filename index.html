Meaningful triage

Bond lets you know what is expected from you, before you start reading anything.

So you always handle what matters, when it matters to you.

Easy lifecycle management

In Bond messages are automatically sorted based upon their completion status.

So you never waste time again unscrambling a pile of messages.

Assignment owner highlight

Bond isolates what you owe, from what you are owed.

So you can choose to get things done, or to track what you are waiting for.

Completion readiness alert

Be notified when inputs are ready, or missing as due time gets close.

So you always respond at the right time, without the tracking hustle.

Contextual insight

In Bond, you know which project or group your messages are related to.

Focus on the desired topic, never navigate through mixed up subjects.

Reading made easy

Bond uses conversational messaging, just like in dialogues.

So you never need to scroll all the way down to catch up with a thread.

How it works

Compose better...

With Bond, senders define their intention towards each recipient: action, question, thread, invite etc. A child can do it, in no time.

...Receive better

In Bond, messages automatically land in the appropriate bucket. The cool thing? Your sender did this, not an Artificial Intelligence.

So you can trust it.

Manage efficiently

New messages land in the inbox. They automatically move to pending when they are read, unless they are marked as complete.

Forget the big pile of messages.

Increase Productivity & Save time

Isn't that great to instantly spot what matters, actions you are assigned, or answers you were waiting for? Set aside lower importance messages without the fear of missing anything, and deliver faster than ever.
